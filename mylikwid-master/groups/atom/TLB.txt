LONG
Formulas:
DTLB misses = DATA_TLB_MISSES_DTLB_MISS
DTLB miss rate = DATA_TLB_MISSES_DTLB_MISS/INSTR_RETIRED_ANY
--
The DTLB miss rate gives a measure how often a TLB miss occurred per instruction.
